About Us

Care that empowers you to take charge of your health and well‑being.

Nakedi Physiotherapy is a young practice with growing experience. Our aim is simple: make physiotherapy accessible to all populations by offering affordable care without compromising on quality.

We provide evidence‑based treatments and compassionate guidance. From the first assessment to your final session, our focus is on restoring function, reducing pain, and helping you move with confidence.

  • Evidence‑based, personalised programs
  • Clear education and home exercises
  • Family‑friendly, accessible pricing

Our Services

Comprehensive physiotherapy for all conditions and ages.

Orthopaedic Manual Therapy

Hands-on care for joints, muscles, and bones to restore mobility and reduce pain.

Hydrotherapy

Water-based rehabilitation in a controlled environment to support recovery after injury or surgery.

Neurological Rehabilitation

Targeted therapy for conditions affecting the nervous system and brain function.

Sports Rehabilitation

Assessment and treatment for sports injuries with return-to-play guidance.

Post-Surgical Rehabilitation

Progressive programs to restore strength, range of motion, and function following surgery.

Pain Management

Evidence-based approaches for chronic and acute pain, tailored to your lifestyle.

Cardiorespiratory Physiotherapy

Treatment for conditions impacting the heart, lungs, and breathing.

Paediatrics Therapy

Gentle, effective care for children with developmental or physical needs.
